Welcome to Suwannee River Rendezvous


Campground & RV Resort

The Suwannee River Rendezvous Resort & Campground is a natural fresh water spring that feed the Suwannee river.


With a 40-acre Campground that has much to offer campers whether is a short stay or long-term. The Lower Rendezvous includes primitive tent sites, water and electric only sites, and FHU 30-amp sites for campers less than 25' in length.


The Upper Rendezvous, also known as the RV Resort has FHU 50-amp site for large campers which includes cable and Wi-Fi.
